ABOUT MARI LAPPIN




I am a Junior Web Developer currently completing a Boot Camp Web Developmemt program at Eleven Fifty Academy in Indianapolis. Much like coding languages, my life has had many versions and updates (including glitches!)

I became interested in Eleven Fifty after a coworker attended a program there. When I was furloughed from my job at a nonprofit due to Covid-19, I found myself at a crossroad. I wanted to seize the opportunity to learn a new skill set or maybe a language, or both. Coding fit the bill perfectly and will also give me the creative outlet I have been seeking.

I am interested in project work or joining a team to expand my experience and abilities. I enjoy providing great customer service and communication.

I have many varied interests and an unquenchable thirst for learning. I love travel, food, photography, gardening, books, my rescue dog and my teenage twins (not necessarily in that order!)

I believe that if you just keep moving forward, life will show you the way.
